Reports

Here Admin, Supervisor and Manager can view and generate reports. Here we can filter the tickets information for selected dates, assigned to, teams (department or group), status and prioity. First filter the tickets as per your requestment and then do export to excel



Comments page Information:

You will redirect to comments page of particular ticket once you click on ticket id or title from home page.

  1. From this page you can assign and update the status of the tickets.

  2. You can reply to the ticket.

  3. Can merge the ticket using Ticket ID.

  4. Can consult to team member before the transfering the Tickets.

  5. Can transfer the ticket from one department to another.

  6. Can add private notes for agents reference.

  7. Can split the ticket.
