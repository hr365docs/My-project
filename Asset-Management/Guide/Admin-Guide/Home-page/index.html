Home Page



Unassigned: Here Admins and Managers will receive all raised tickets, from here they can assign tickets to agents and supervisor.

Team Tickets: Here Agents and Supervisors can see tickets those assigned to them and their team members

Teams Resolved: Here Agents or Supervisors can see tickets those tickets have been resolved (Ticket's status is updated to resolved.

Teams Closed Ticket: Here Agents or Supervisors can see tickets those tickets have been closed. (Ticket's status is updated to closed)

Ticket Mailbox: The tickets raised from help desk email ID will fall under this, Admin can view such tickets. You can give access to view ticket mailbox to Ticket Mail Manager from settings.

My Tickets: Here agent or supervisor can see tickets only when assigned to them.

Requested by Me: Admin, Manager, supervisor, and agents can see their raised tickets in this tab.

My Resolved: Admin, Manager, supervisor, and agents can see their tickets those have been resolved.

Note: Admin can enable or disable this tab from custom settings.

My Closed: Admin, Manager, supervisor, and agents can see their tickets those have been closed
